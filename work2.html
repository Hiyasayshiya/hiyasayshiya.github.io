002 : Weave Design Project

Weave Design- Architectural Ichor

Mentor- Dr. Mohammad Javed

CONCEPT Sustainable weave design project using leftover materials.The main idea is to complete a structure. Taking an example of architecture (structure) and blood (inner portion). "For every house is incomplete when no one lives there". Inspiration is taken from the bold geometric shapes in architectural buildings, balconies and the windows and juxtaposing it with the fluidity of blood and coming up with abstract geometric compositions in form of weave using conventional and unconventional materials. 'Ichor' here isn't synonymous to blood, but metaphorical to the people living in harmony under the buildings.


OBJECTIVEThe sole objective is to explore different ideas to make swatches on cardboard loom in reference to the derivatives learnt in class incorporating creative ideas based on the theme taken.

Weave Look

Ingredient-Leftover Pasta and plastic rope

Ingredient- Ice-cream Sticks
